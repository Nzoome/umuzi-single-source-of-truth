# What is Manco and Why Does It Exist?

You may start seeing references to **Manco** (short for Management Committee). Here’s what that means and why we’re introducing it.

## New to Manco?

Here is the 2026 Welcome Pack outlining expectations and deliverables.

## What is Manco?

Manco is a senior management committee responsible for the day-to-day running of the organisation. Its role is to make sure work moves forward smoothly, teams are supported, and operational issues are dealt with quickly and clearly.

## What Does Manco Focus On?

Manco focuses on:

- Turning strategy into action
- Coordinating work across teams
- Improving how we work (processes, tools, ways of working)
- Making sure resources are used well
- Removing blockers that slow teams down

In short: helping teams do their best work, with fewer obstacles.

## How is Manco Different from Exco?

- **Exco** focuses on strategy, direction, and long-term priorities.
- **Manco** focuses on execution, delivery, and operations.

This separation helps decisions happen at the right level and keeps things moving.

## What Does This Mean for You?

- Clearer decision-making and escalation paths
- Faster resolution of cross-team issues
- Less uncertainty about who owns what
- More support to deliver work effectively

Manco is not about adding layers — it’s about creating clarity, focus, and flow.

> If you’re ever unsure whether something should be escalated, raised, or improved, your manager or Manco members are there to help.
