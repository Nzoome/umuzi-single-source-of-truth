## Staff Resourcing + Recruitment Process

### Resources
- Job Description Template 
- Job Descriptions Google Drive 
- Staff Resourcing Request Form

### Signing off on role, budget and timeline
Hiring manager to inform CEO/EXCO of the resource/role needed and discuss the budget and timeline for recruitment. It is a good idea to have a Job Description (JD) in mind prior to a conversation with senior leadership. 

Once the role has been signed off, create and finalise the JD using this template. Make sure to include **Umuzi** cultures and the appropriate 'How To Apply' section. All JDs should include this Monday form in the 'How To Apply' section, as can be found on the template. Please save the new JD to this Google Drive folder.

> The Operations team will make sure to add the new role to the list of options on the form.

### Create a Skills Test (ST) if relevant for the role 

### Checklist before handing over to Operations Team:
1. Role signed off
2. Budget confirmed 
3. Timelines confirmed 
4. Job Description finalised + Skills Test if necessary 

### Notifying the Operations Team of the new role
As a final step, fill out this form, with all necessary information to hand over to the Operations team.
