# What is Exco and Why Does It Exist?

You may also see references to Exco (short for Executive Committee). Here’s what Exco is responsible for and how it fits into how we work.

## What is Exco?

Exco is the organisation’s strategic leadership committee. Its role is to set direction, ensure long-term sustainability, and provide oversight for Umuzi as a whole.

## What Does Exco Focus On?

Exco focuses on:

- Organisational strategy and long-term priorities
- Ensuring alignment with Umuzi’s mission and impact goals
- Governance, risk, and major policy decisions
- Big-picture financial and organisational decisions

In short: where we’re going and why.

## How is Exco Different from Manco?

- Exco focuses on strategy, direction, and oversight.
- Manco focuses on execution, operations, and delivery.

This allows Exco to stay focused on the future, while Manco ensures the work gets done effectively day to day.

## What Does This Mean for You?

- Clear strategic direction and priorities
- Better alignment across the organisation
- Fewer last-minute changes due to clearer upfront decisions
- Confidence that day-to-day decisions are supported by a strong strategic foundation

Exco exists to set the direction and create the conditions for the organisation to thrive, while trusting teams and Manco to deliver.
