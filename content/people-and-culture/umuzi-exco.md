# Umuzi EXCO

## Lyndi Lawson-Smith

As CEO, Lyndi leads the organisation with a clear vision and deep commitment to impact. She is responsible for guiding the overall strategy and ensuring that every programme, partnership, and initiative moves us closer to our mission. Lyndi shapes the culture of accountability, empathy, and excellence that defines our work. ## Andrew Levy

Andrew is the co-founder of Umuzi and a passionate advocate for unlocking young talent globally. Driven by a deep belief that investing in people builds stronger societies, Andrew founded Umuzi to nurture a new generation of thinkers, creators, and problem solvers who can transform the trajectory of our country and continent. His vision of social capital as a powerful force for change continues to shape the way Umuzi approaches learning, leadership, and impact. Through his work, Andrew inspires a culture of innovation, empathy, and purpose reminding us that when young people are given the tools and trust to lead, they can change everything. Andrew believes the power to change the world has always been within us and he's here to help us unleash it.
