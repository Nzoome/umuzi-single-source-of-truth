# Staff Leave at Umuzi

## Have You Read Our Staff Leave Policy Yet?

Any leave you are applying for should be done on **SageHR**. This includes:
- Annual leave
- Sick leave
- Birthday leave
- Family responsibility leave
- Maternity & paternity leave
- Adoption leave
- Commission leave

Here is the comprehensive Staff Leave Policy. Below are some basic steps on how to apply for your different leave.

## How To Apply for Leave

When a staff member would like to apply for leave (annual leave, sick leave, study leave, or family responsibility leave), there are a few steps to follow:

1. Speak to your manager about taking leave.
2. Apply for leave using SageHR. You can do so from your Dashboard by clicking on the "+ Request time off" button.
3. Your leave will automatically reflect on the Umuzi Annual calendar.

## Leave Preparation: Key Admin Tasks

- Make sure to draft a list of your responsibilities and who will be handling what. This visibility is important so that things can be handled appropriately in your absence.
- Turn on your out of office on your emails. Include when you will be back from your leave and who to contact for assistance in your absence.
- Have an "Out of Office" on your calendar for the days that you'll be on leave.
- Decline any meetings you will miss while on leave.

## Sick Leave Process

If you are unable to work due to illness, please follow the steps below:

1. Notify your manager by 08h30 on the day of your absence.
2. Log your sick leave on SageHR as soon as possible.

A valid medical certificate is required if:
- You are absent for 2 or more consecutive days,
- You are absent more than twice within an 8-week period, or
- Your sick leave is taken adjacent to a weekend or public holiday.

> **Tip:** Download the SageHR app so you can quickly log sick leave from your phone. This also ensures that the rest of the organisation can see that you're off sick, helping teams plan and adjust accordingly.

> **Reminder:** Full-time employees are entitled to 30 days of paid sick leave over a 3-year cycle (in line with South African labour law). Please use your leave responsibly and refer to the Leave Policy for full details and guidelines.

## Some Important Things To Remember

- As we’re a South African-based organisation, all team members — regardless of location — receive South African public holidays as standard.
- If you're based outside of South Africa and would like to observe public holidays in your own country, you're welcome to do so, but you'll need to apply for annual leave for those days. This helps ensure consistency and alignment across the team while still allowing space for individual observances.
- Apply for leave within the correct time frame:
  - When applying for up to 2 days leave, apply with at least 1 week's notice.
  - When applying for 3 - 5 days leave, apply with at least 2 weeks' notice.
  - When applying for more than 5 days leave, apply with at least 1 month's notice.

When it comes to sick leave, you must inform your manager ASAP and you will be required to complete a leave form upon returning to work.

## How Do You Know If Your Leave Is Approved?

- Discuss your planned leave with your manager before submitting the request on SageHR.
- Apply for leave in advance, so there is enough time for it to be reviewed and approved before your intended start date.
- You will receive a notification from SageHR once your manager has approved your leave.
- If you haven’t received confirmation, follow up directly with your manager to ensure your leave has been processed and approved.

## What If You Need To Cancel/Change Your Leave Request?

When a staff member would like to cancel or change their leave request, here are the steps to follow (please do discuss this with your manager as well):

### Cancelling your leave

1. On your SageHR dashboard, find the Upcoming time off widget.
2. Next to your time off request, please click the red X.
3. Please enter a message as to why you are cancelling it, then click Cancel Time off request.

### Changing your leave

1. On your SageHR dashboard, find the Upcoming time off widget.
2. Next to your time off request, click the green pencil icon, make your changes, then click Submit changes.

Your manager will then need to approve these changes. Please note you can still make changes even if the request is already approved; however, it will send out a new approval request.
