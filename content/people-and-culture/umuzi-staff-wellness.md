# Umuzi Staff Wellness

At Umuzi, we believe in the importance of building a culture where every team member feels fully supported. One that puts the holistic well-being of our people first. Where taking care of your mental health is seen as a sign of strength.

## Partnership with Ollie Health

To help us better support you, we have partnered with **Ollie Health**. Ollie is an online mental wellness platform that provides:

- 1:1 therapy sessions
- Group therapy sessions

With a range of different types of licensed therapists and various group sessions. It is private and secure. All virtual therapy sessions are encrypted, and a unique private meeting link is generated for each new session. None of the content from your sessions will be reported back to us at any point.

## Access to Virtual Credit Wallet

All team members now have access to a virtual credit wallet. These credits allow you to book sessions with a counselor from anywhere, anonymously.

### Here’s the deal:

1. You will receive an email from Ollie with your username and password.
2. You will be able to log in to choose a therapist (Ollie helper) that suits your needs and schedule a session as and when needed.
3. If you experience any issues, please reach out to [natalie.jorgensen@umuzi.org](mailto:natalie.jorgensen@umuzi.org).
