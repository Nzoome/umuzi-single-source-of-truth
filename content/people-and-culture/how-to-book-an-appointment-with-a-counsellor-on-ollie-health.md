# How to Book an Appointment with a Counsellor on Ollie Health

As the world gets more and more fast-paced, and our demands on both ourselves and what we want grow, a healthy look at one's well-being is vitally important. To help us better support you, we have partnered with **Ollie Health**.

## Who is Ollie?

Ollie is an online mental wellness platform that provides:

- 1:1 sessions
- Group pods
- A range of different types of licensed Counsellors

It is private and secure. All virtual counselling sessions are encrypted, and a unique private meeting link is generated for each new session. All Umuzi staff now have access to a virtual credit wallet. These credits allow you to book sessions with a counsellor from anywhere, anonymously.

## How to Book a Session

1. Search for an email from Ollie with your username and password in your inbox.
2. Log in and choose a Counsellor that specializes in your needs and schedule a session.
3. After each session, you will see the remaining balance of your virtual credits wallet on your profile.

> If you need support, please reach out to Liezel for assistance.
