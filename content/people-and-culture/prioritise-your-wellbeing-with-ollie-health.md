## 🧠 What is Ollie?

Ollie Health is an online mental wellness platform that offers:
- Private 1:1 therapy with licensed professionals
- Group therapy and topic-based group sessions
- A variety of counsellors with different backgrounds and specialties
- A safe, encrypted platform — totally private and secure

And here’s the best part, your sessions are completely confidential. None of the content will be shared with Umuzi, ever.

## 🪙 Virtual Credit Wallet – How It Works

All Umuzi team members have access to a single shared virtual credit wallet. This means the credits are not unique to each person but instead reflect the total amount available for all team members. When you book a session, credits will be deducted from this shared wallet.

> Never worry about an empty wallet; this is just how the system works, and there will always be enough to support you.

## 💻 How to Book Your Session

Booking is quick and easy! Just follow these steps:
1. Login to Ollie. You would’ve received your login details from Ollie when you joined Umuzi. 
   > (Can’t find it? Search “Ollie Wealth” in your inbox or check with Natalie.)
2. Head to the “Book your Health Expert” section on the platform.
3. Browse the available experts and choose someone you feel comfortable with.
4. Decide between a 30- or 60-minute session, then select a date and time.
5. You’ll receive a confirmation email with a unique, private meeting link.

> Add it to Your Calendar.
