# Contract Renewals

*Please note the contract renewal process will be done solely on Sage HR:*

When a staff member's contract for employment is coming to an end, the People Function is responsible for informing that person's manager (the person that the employee reports to) at least **3 months** before the contract ends. This is an automation that has been set up on SageHR, to inform the People Function of this deadline.

The below process will then be followed for the person whose contract is up for renewal. This process will be led by the People Function, with input from the person's manager:

## Performance

A performance review process with the staff member is to be completed on Sage HR. The review form will be sent to, at a minimum, the manager, staff member, and a peer to give a **360 feedback review**. The person or their manager can request for other people that they have worked with closely to also provide feedback, including people that report to them.

The categories of performance that will be assessed should line up with a person's job description as much as possible. Going forward, the person should see the performance review metrics before they will be assessed using this method, so that there is clarity around what is expected of them in their role. This could include areas such as:
- Technical expertise
- Delivery & outputs
- Professional conduct and communication
- Upskilling and contribution to culture

The staff member will also rate themselves on the above categories. The rating scale is as follows:
1. Far below expectations
2. Below expectations
3. Meets expectations
4. Exceeds expectations

As with the other reviews, the staff member can make comments or explain the score that they've given themselves.

Once everyone has completed their reviews, a detailed report will be sent to the person’s manager, and the manager will be able to talk to the staff member about any areas where there are differences in opinion around performance between their self-review and the review from others.

Identify both areas where they are doing exceptionally well and a few areas for improvement.

The People Function will provide deadlines for completion of the Performance Reviews - ideally to be completed **3 weeks** before the contract expires.

## Considerations for Contract Renewal

Reflect on the role that this person has played over the duration of their current contract. Look at whether there is still a need for this role in Umuzi going forward - do you need to look at changing this person's role/title? Has this person contributed meaningfully to the point of promotion? (use Performance Review in step 1 above to help determine this).

Keep in mind this great point by Adam Grant: 
> "It's easier to fill gaps in competence than gaps in character. Even if people have strong experience, it's a mistake to hire or promote them if they have weak values. Knowledge and skills can be learned. Principles like integrity, humility, and generosity are tougher to teach."

As best as possible, talk about possible career paths for the staff member at Umuzi - what work would the employee enjoy doing more of? Where do they see their strengths lying? Taking the vision of Umuzi into account, how do you see the staff member fitting into the future of our organisation?

## Increase Process

The People Function will then use the reviews, as well as historical data on the employee's salary, and any external benchmarking, working together with the person's manager to put forward a suggestion to the remuneration committee for increase/promotion or change of title. The remuneration committee will be part of the quarterly board meetings of Umuzi.

The Remuneration Committee will then meet and decide on an appropriate increase amount, taking budget into account. When considering the increase to be put forward as well as the decision by the remuneration committee, the below factors should be taken into account:
- Inflation
- Increase in responsibility/promotion/change of role
- Industry norms or available information on earning potential for this person at this level, outside of Umuzi. Types of resources that can be useful for this include:
  - Ad Talent 2021 Salary Survey
  - Offerzen 2022 Careers Report

This will be communicated to the People Function and the People Function will inform the employee, their manager, and finance of the increase. The People Function will then draw up a new contract on SageHR, sending it to the employee and the employee's manager.

## Contract Meeting with the Staff Member

Once the new contract is created with the approved remuneration, the People Function will send the contract on Sage HR to the manager and staff member informing everyone via email. The manager will meet with the staff member to discuss the new contract and give an opportunity to raise any questions or concerns that the staff member may have regarding the new contract.

If there are no concerns, the manager and staff member will sign (on Sage HR) before the end of the current contract.

## Measuring Success

Once the manager has the information from their Performance review (1 above) and their role going forward (2), they will work together with the staff member to set goals. This can be done within Sage HR in the Goals section, please refer to this link to show you how to set it up in Sage HR Goals & OKRs overview.

You can link it back to the metrics used in the Final performance reviews report, especially if there were big areas for improvement. Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) are a nice way to do this. Set up to **3 objectives** for this person to focus on within a time frame and then identify ways that you'll measure whether or not these objectives are being met (key results).
